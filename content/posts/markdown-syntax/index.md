## Headings

there is 6 levels of headings in markdown, from `#` to `######` : `<h1>` to `<h6>`

`# H1` : `## H2` : `## H2` : `### H3` : `#### H4` : `##### H5` : `###### H6`

```markdown
# H1 or H1

        =

## H2 or H2

         ==

### H3 or H3

          ===

#### H4 or H4

           ====

##### H5 or H5

            =====

###### H6 or H6

             ======
```
